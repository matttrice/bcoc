# About Berthoud Church of Christ

We are a welcoming community of believers located in Berthoud, Colorado, at the foot of the beautiful Rocky Mountains. Our congregation is united by a shared commitment to follow the teachings of Jesus Christ.

## Our Mission

We exist to:

- **Worship God** in spirit and in truth
- **Study His Word** together and grow in faith
- **Serve** with love and compassion
- **Share the Gospel** with those seeking truth

## What We Believe

We are members of the Church that Jesus built (Matthew 16:18). We follow the Bible as our sole guide for salvation, faith and practice, seeking to implement the New Testament pattern of worship which has its origins in Old Testament patterns.

Peter, recpient of the keys "of the kingdom of heaven" from Jesus tells us what saves today:

> **1 Peter 3:18-21**  
> For Christ also died for sins once for all, the just for the unjust, so that He might bring us to God, having been put to death in the flesh, but made alive in the spirit;
 ...     
> Corresponding to that, baptism now saves you—not the removal of dirt from the flesh, but an appeal to God for a good conscience—through the resurrection of Jesus Christ.

The keys of that message are inherited by the Church and we teach the same prescription for salvation. If you are seeking the **true message** from God, please reach out to us for an online or in-person study. If you are already a seasoned bible student, our approach will organize and uniify what you already know. If you are brand-new to the Bible, we will help you take those first steps to understanding God's word as it relates to the journey of life. 

## What to Expect

When you visit, you'll find:

- **Warm, friendly people** glad to see you
- **Bible-centered** teaching and shared duties
- **A cappella singing** that lifts the spirit
- **The Lord's Supper** observed every Sunday
- **No pressure** — come as you are

## Our Community

Berthoud is a wonderful small town in Northern Colorado with stunning mountain views and easy access to the entire front-range.

We'd love for you to visit us and see for yourself what we're all about. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out!
